Sharing the Passion of Fire

In this space we share creations and practices related to the fire element which evoke passion to continue doing what you love to do. You are welcome to send your creative expressions such as photos (below 5MB), short poems, art work, short prose, and short videos (up to 2 minutes). Please mention your location.

Send to:
creativeresilience@khyentsefoundation.org

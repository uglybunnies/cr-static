Sharing the Beauty of Space

This is a place where we share our inner and outer experiences of becoming spacious like the sky. You are most welcome to send your experiences in the form of photos (below 5MB), short poems, artwork, short videos (up to 2 minutes), and brief writings. Please mention your location.

Send to:
creativeresilience@khyentsefoundation.org
